About This Project

This AI Interview Simulator was designed to help job seekers practice for interviews in a modern, interactive way.

Features:

  • Dynamic question generation based on job domain and difficulty.
  • Real-time posture and eye-contact analysis using your webcam.
  • Voice-to-text for answering questions naturally.
  • In-depth evaluation of answers powered by Google's Gemini AI.
  • Progress tracking and interview history to monitor your improvement.
